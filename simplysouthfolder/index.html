Welcome to Simply
South

We started in May 2009. Started by Shankar Shrinivas and Rashi Shrinivas It is located in greater noida which is city having a large institutional area comprising of several colleges, schools and university. He himself was a student of a one of the colleges in the same institutional area. Keeping this is mind we simply south got started which can cater to students. In 2012 this restaurant became a 35 seater and a further expansion in 2014 has helped it become a 60 seater restaurant today. We are the first exclusive south Indian restaurant in Greater noida.
